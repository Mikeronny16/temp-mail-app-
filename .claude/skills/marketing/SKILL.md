# Skill: marketing

Growth & marketing for SE Asia + global creators. Myanmar-first context.

## Launch Checklist

### Pre-Launch (1 week before)
- [ ] Landing page live with email capture
- [ ] Waitlist with social share ("I just joined — join me")
- [ ] Teaser post on Facebook (Myanmar primary) + TikTok
- [ ] 5 beta users lined up for testimonials

### Launch Day
- [ ] Post in Myanmar freelancer Facebook groups
- [ ] Product Hunt submit (for global reach)
- [ ] TikTok/Reels demo video (60s, Burmese voiceover)
- [ ] WhatsApp broadcast to existing contacts

### Post-Launch
- [ ] Follow up beta users for testimonial screenshots
- [ ] Update landing page with real numbers
- [ ] Week 2: tutorial content ("How I used X to...")

## Myanmar Channels (ranked by reach)
1. **Facebook Groups** — Myanmar Freelancers, Digital Marketing Myanmar
2. **Messenger** — direct outreach, still dominant
3. **TikTok** — fastest growing, under-25 demographic
4. **Telegram** — tech-savvy users, developer groups
5. **LinkedIn** — small but high-value for B2B

## Viral Mechanics to Build In
```
Share card    — downloadable PNG with their stats/result
Leaderboard   — public ranking drives competition  
Streak badge  — 🔥 7-day streak = social share trigger
Referral link — "Give 5 credits, get 5 credits"
```
